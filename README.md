# LIRI-Bot

** run node Liri 

** select an option
    *** Concert-this
    *** Spotify this song
    *** movie-this 

** hit enter

** For concert-this enter Artist or band
** For Spotify-this-song pick a song
** For movie-this pick a movie 

** hit enter

**then enter y for yes or for no

** hit enter and get the information returned
